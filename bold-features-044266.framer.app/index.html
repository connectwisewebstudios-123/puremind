FAQ

Questions Answered

If you don't see your question answered here, please get in touch.

What can I expect from a session?

Sessions are tailored to your needs and may include deep listening, guided reflection, meditation, or energy work. You’ll have a safe and supportive space to explore your emotions, challenges, and spiritual journey.

How do I know if spiritual counselling is right for me?

If you're feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or searching for deeper meaning in your life, spiritual counselling can help. It’s for anyone who wants emotional healing, clarity, or personal transformation.

How long is a typical session?

Sessions typically last between 60 to 90 minutes, depending on your needs. Retreats and workshops may have different timeframes.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes! Spiritual counselling and energy healing sessions are available both in-person and online, so you can receive support no matter where you are.
